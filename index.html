Information Collected

I collect the following information in order to create whitepapers for the education of the analytics community.

You also consent to me submitting a portion of the data which I will obtain from Adobe on your behalf. When information is hashed, it will be done with the PBKDF2 algorithm (the same algorithm recommended and used to protect passwords), using a salt and 5000 iterations. This hashing system will protect your identifying details for decades to come. I will collect the following information:

  • The hash of the filename you used.
  • A hash of the organization name. This is used to identify usage by names. The name of your organization may also be directly captured.
  • A hash of your user name. Your username is not captured directly.
  • The total number of report suites in your organization. The titles and rsids of these reportsuites are not captured.
  • For each report suite you select to include in the report, I also collect the following:
    • How many eVars, props, and events are enabled.
    • How many eVars, props, and events have descriptions.
    • How many eVars, props, and events have names.
    • How many eVars, props, and events have names but which are not enabled.
    • The numbers of enabled eVars, props, and events.
    • The slot numbers (e.g. eVar2, prop4) of configured eVars, props, and events.
